LEWIS HAMILTON

Seven-time world champion, the greatest F1 driver is also one of the biggest political figures today, a protagonist in a sport considered by many as extremely elitist and "apolitical", Hamilton seems to be the exception to the rule. Having publicly expressed support for movements fighting for the rights of blacks and women, he is also an environmental activist and demands more sustainability from F1 through the reduction of carbon emissions. "The world is a disaster and its leaders are not well-educated or unconcerned about environmental issues," he said. Hamilton's constant positions using his influence to support important causes demonstrate that he is a true champion, both on and off the racetracks.
