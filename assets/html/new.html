Formula 1

Formula 1 is the highest class of motor racing in the world. It organizes an international series of races on round tracks on various continents. In these competitions, the most talented drivers compete against the largest car manufacturers. Formula 1 is known for its exciting speeds, advanced technology and dynamic track competition, attracting millions of fans from all over the world.
